HISTORY/BREWING

Guinness Family

In 1764 Alfred Guinness married his wife Olivia Whitmore and continued to have 21 children together however only 10 managed to survive.

You can read more about this here: (https://astrumpeople.com/arthur-guinness-biography/)

Arther Guinness was born on the 24th of September 1725 into the privileged household of the Guinness family in County Kildare, just west of Dublin. When Arther reached 20 years of age, his godfather passed away and left him and his father €100 This may not seem like a lot but €100 was the equivalent of 4 years of work. This meant that at a young age, he had a lot of money to spend and settled on starting a little brewery with his father. After the brand started to take off, the business grew and he moved into the centre of Dublin. The brewery started making ales but after a few different recipes, the public fell in love with the stout.
