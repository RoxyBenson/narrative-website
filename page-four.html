My Recommendations

My personal favourite Guinness spots around Manchester including recommendations depending who your with or the type of night you fancy.

  • 1

    Mulligans

    Mulligans

    Found in the heart of Manchester, Mulligans (found in Deansgate) is the closest thing to a good old pint of Irish Guinness. Great pints that are perfect after a refreshing day, with a nice and bustling pub. Its a great place to take friends and family although more on the expencive you will have guarented great night.

  • 2

    The Footage

    Are you a student? Then this is the place for you. Great pints at a student discount and its right in the middle of town. Always full with students you are able to relax and socialise while playing a few rounds of pool. Its only a short walk back to accomidation (MMU) and always has pleanty of entertainments on so is a great place to watch the rugby on a Saturday night!

  • 3

    Albert Schloss

    Albert Schloss is not for the faint hearted. A great please to party and have fun if your not on a budget. Ram packed and busy they have live soul singers that will get you dancing on the tables. The Guinness they serve is a good qualuty and therefore one of my faves, although it may not come in a Guinness glass! A great place to go to with friends and family however i dont recommend going if you have a headache!

  • 4

    Salutaions

    Great pub that is located in the middle of the MMU Campus, helping to quench your thirst after a long day of lecutres. Great place to catch up with mates, this is a small and secluded pun with little space outside. Its perfect for the one on one catchup you need, providing great food and drinks.
